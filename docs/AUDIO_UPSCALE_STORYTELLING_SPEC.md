# Audio, Upscale, and Storytelling Specification

## Storytelling

Storytelling is mandatory across all domains, especially clips.

### Architecture story arcs

- Arrival reveal
- Day-to-night transformation
- Construction-to-finished transition
- Material detail journey
- Human experience walkthrough
- Before/after redesign
- Urban context reveal

### Photography story arcs

- Product reveal
- Texture/macro discovery
- Use-case lifestyle moment
- Luxury ritual
- Seasonal campaign moment
- Before/after set transformation

### Branding story arcs

- Brand launch reveal
- Identity evolution
- Campaign narrative
- Product promise
- Real estate lifestyle aspiration
- Heritage-to-modern transformation

## Audio background generation

Clip scenarios should support:

- Audio required true/false
- Audio mood
- Music genre
- Tempo
- Instrumentation
- Ambience
- SFX direction
- Voiceover usage
- Voiceover tone
- Audio prompt

## Upscale

Upscale should be available after:

- image generation
- clip generation
- imported asset upload

Upscale modes:

- clean upscale
- photoreal detail enhance
- architectural detail enhance
- product sharpness enhance
- brand/ad export enhance
- denoise
- face-safe enhance
- video sharpen
- social export resize
- print export resize
